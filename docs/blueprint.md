# **App Name**: Arisan Ngamin

## Core Features:

- Arisan Group Management: Create and manage multiple arisan groups, each representing a different cycle or purpose within the family.
- Member Management: Add, edit, and remove family members participating in the arisan, tracking their contact information and participation status.
- Payment Tracking: Log and track arisan payments for each member, automatically calculating due dates and sending reminders. AI tool helps determine when and how frequently to send remainders to each family member to promote on-time payments
- Winner Selection: Implement a fair and transparent method for selecting the arisan winner each cycle, such as a random drawing or lottery system.
- Financial Reporting: Generate reports summarizing the arisan's financial status, including total contributions, payouts, and outstanding balances.
- Notifications: Automatically send notifications to remind members of upcoming payment deadlines.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep orange (#D97706) to evoke warmth and a sense of family.
- Background color: Dark charcoal (#232323) for a professional and elegant look.
- Accent color: Light orange (#F2A950) for call-to-action buttons and important notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif,  for readability and a modern look.
- Use clear, minimalist icons to represent different functions and categories within the app.
- Prioritize a clean, intuitive layout with clear sections and straightforward navigation for ease of use.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance the user experience and provide visual feedback.
